How sure is this? Phone GPS is only accurate to ~5–30 ft — and worse against big walls (Yosemite!) where rock blocks the signal. The shaded circle on the map is your GPS margin of error, so the right boulder is whichever pin sits in it. Tap a crag to see its routes and confirm by eye. Auto-identifying the exact route from the image itself is a future feature.
